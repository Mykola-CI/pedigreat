About Our Team

Welcome to our world of pet companionship! We are a team of dedicated dog lovers, committed to providing exceptional care for your furry family members. Our services range from dog boarding and sitting, to training, walking, and offering professional advice on all aspects of dog keeping and breeding.

We understand that your dogs are more than just pets - they are part of your family. That's why we treat every dog in our care as if they were our own, providing them with the love, attention, and care they deserve.

Vacation Home Visits

Whether you're away on vacation, out of town for business, or simply have a busy schedule, our Home Visits service is here to ensure your pet's needs are met in the comfort of their own home. With our Home Visits, you can have peace of mind knowing your pets are in safe and loving hands. Our dedicated team will handle feeding, playtime, walks, and any necessary medication, ensuring your pets are well-cared for in your absence.

Dog Training

Our positive reinforcement dog training focuses on achieving the specific behaviors you want to see in your dog. We take the time to understand your goals and map out a customized training plan.
